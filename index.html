The playbook

Plan.
Build.
Ship.

A practical framework for connecting AI assistants, developer platforms, and everyday SaaS products across the software development lifecycle.

  1. 01

    Give AI useful context

    Organise project knowledge, constraints, and instructions so your tools can produce relevant work instead of generic output.

  2. 02

    Match tools to the work

    Use popular AI tools, developer services, and SaaS platforms where each one adds real leverage—from research and planning to coding and review.

  3. 03

    Build repeatable workflows

    Turn useful techniques into clear routines you can apply every day, without rebuilding your process for every task.

  4. 04

    Keep judgement in the loop

    Verify outputs, protect sensitive context, and adapt the system as the tools and your work evolve.
